Understanding Fish Tiers & Locations

Fish in WEBFISHING are categorized by tiers, representing their rarity and difficulty to catch:

Special Guide: Catching the UFO

The UFO is one of WEBFISHING's most elusive catches, requiring specific conditions and quick reactions to obtain:

Spawn Conditions

  • Only appears in the five lakes and rivers on the map
  • Spawns randomly, announced by a distinct explosion or thud sound
  • Appears as a brown, radiation-emitting object in the water
  • Despawns after 60 seconds if not caught

Hunting Tips

  • Play with game sound on and avoid background music
  • Position yourself centrally on the island for better sound detection
  • Consider playing on a private server to avoid competition
  • React quickly - you have just one minute to locate and catch it

Important Note

In multiplayer sessions, the UFO can only be caught by one player. The first to reach and successfully fish it claims the prize.
